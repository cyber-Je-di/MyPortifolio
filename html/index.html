About Me

Hi there! I'm Tembo Madalitso, a passionate and driven final-year Computer Science student at Mulungushi University. With a deep interest in cybersecurity, cloud computing, and software development, I enjoy tackling challenges that require innovative solutions. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m actively expanding my expertise in backend technologies and cloud forensics. Beyond academics, I lead as the President of the ICTAZ Mulungushi University Chapter, where I foster growth and collaboration among students in technology. My experiences in hackathons, Capture The Flag competitions, and digital forensics have fueled my ambition to make meaningful contributions to the tech world. I'm excited about leveraging my technical and leadership skills to create impactful projects that inspire and innovate.
